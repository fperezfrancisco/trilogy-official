Who We Are

Here at Trilogy, we are a private soccer training service based in Santa Clarita, CA, created to help soccer players develop effectively. Founded by Coach Francisco J. Perez, the vision for Trilogy Soccer has always been based on the belief that talent isn’t something you’re born with, but a result of hard-work, diligence, discipline, and consistency. If you have the drive to become the best soccer athlete, our mission is to help you get there. We take our work seriously and demonstrate that through detail-oriented training sessions that work on all areas of the game -- ball control, passing, receiving, dribbling, shooting, attacking, defending, and more.

Our Training Philosophy

Prepare - Perform -Perfect. Our philosophy promotes a mentality of consistency, discipline, initiative, and intensity. We don’t focus on flashy drills or drills that may be “social media worthy” but rather on game-like specific moments that will transform you into an effective player on the pitch. Our 3-step philosophy focuses on perfecting the fundamentals - the actions that our athletes execute most often on the field (passing, receiving, dribbling, shooting, finishing, speed, and agility). We then build up to performing these actions at game speed and under pressure in our group sessions/camps and we help our athletes understand how to apply the skills they’ve learned in real game-like situations. Whether you’re playing at a high level (academy, college, etc.) or looking to take a step above recreational into the more serious environment of soccer, we have the perfect training for you!

Prepare

Our first step in the journey. The more you prepare and the better you do, the easier it will be to succeed come game time. Put in the hours with diligence, dedication, and intensity, and see how your development journey begins to improve!

Perform

The second step and crucial one. The sole reason all athletes go above and beyond to prepare in the first place is to perform at the highest possible level. At speed, under pressure, and in all conditions, you must learn how to step up and execute in order to succeed. Showcase yourself with confidence.

Perfect

The last step which separates the greats from the rest. Be self-critical and analyze your preparation and performances in order to perfect your process. Pin-point your areas of improvement and build on your areas of strength, but always look to perfect your craft. Never get complacent. Play & refine!
